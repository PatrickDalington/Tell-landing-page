About

It all began with my sister,Amara. I watched my younger sister grow from a novice writer to one who could create alluring stories. But,they got her nowhere. She struggled to find her identity in the numerous storytelling platforms out there. That broke my heart and that's how we began the creation of TELLIT.

One sunny afternoon in the year 2023,, as I sat pondering over ways I could bridge the gap for aspiring writers like Amara, a brilliant idea illuminated my mind like a lightbulb. I envisioned a revolutionary app that would serve as a virtual oasis for writers, providing them with a stage to share their literary creations with the world. Creations of varying kinds to include screenplays, skits scripts, and other interesting forms of writing.

One idea gave birth to many others. Now it's not going to be just the regular stories Amara writes,but writers of all kinds can showcase their branch of literature on this app which would offer them a platform to express their creativity, connect with fellow writers, and possibly even earn recognition and income from their work.

The above fuel my mission to bring Tellit to life and so daily,I put in dedication and hardwork so as to make a grand debut of TELLIT in eager anticipation of a great influx of writers who will interact with the app's interface,explore possibilities in future version updates,and overall,happily share their stories with a global audience.
